In the morning when thou risest unwillingly, let this thought be present:
I am rising to the work of a human being. Why then am I dissatisfied if I
am going to do the things for which I exist and for which I was brought
into the world?

Thou must be like a promontory of the sea, against which, though the waves
beat continually, yet it both itself stands, and about it are those swelling
waves stilled and quieted.

Be not disturbed about the future, for if ever thou shalt come to it, thou
wilt have the same reason for thy guide which preserves thee at present.

Do not act as if thou wert going to live ten thousand years. While thou
livest, while it is in thy power, be good.

If it is not right, do not do it; if it is not true, do not say it.

The art of life is more like the wrestler's art than the dancer's, in
respect of this, that it should stand ready and firm to meet onsets which
are sudden and unexpected.

That which is a hindrance is made a furtherance to an act; and that which
is an obstacle on the road helps us on this road.
