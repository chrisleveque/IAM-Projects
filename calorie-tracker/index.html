    Online food lookup optional

    Off by default. When enabled: barcode lookup sends only the barcode number to Open Food Facts (a free, open, non-profit database), and text search sends only the words you type to the USDA FoodData Central database (a free U.S. government service; needs a free API key). Your profile, weight, food log, and identity are never sent anywhere. Foods you pick are saved to your device for offline reuse.

    Your data

    Everything is stored only in this browser. Back it up or move it with export/import.
